Emergency Guidebook

Search for life-saving instructions during disasters or pandemics.

Heatwave

  • Drink ORS or salted lemon water every hour.
  • Move livestock to shaded, ventilated sheds immediately.
  • Keep windows covered with damp curtains to cool rooms.
  • Avoid high-protein food and alcohol during peak heat.

Floods

  • Store dry food, grains, and seeds on elevated platforms.
  • Turn off the main electrical switch to avoid short circuits.
  • Do not walk through moving water (even 6 inches can knock you over).
  • Keep a 'Go-Bag' ready with ID cards and emergency medicine.

Pandemics

  • Wash hands with soap for at least 20 seconds frequently.
  • Isolate symptomatic family members in a ventilated room.
  • Avoid sharing utensils or towels with someone who is ill.
  • Stay updated via official government radio or SMS alerts.
