Safety Protocols

Heatwave Precautions

  • Drink ORS or Lemon water frequently.
  • Cover windows with jute curtains (Rural cooling).
  • Provide extra water to cattle and pets.

Flood/Heavy Rain

  • Keep an emergency radio and torch ready.
  • Identify the highest geographical point in your village.
  • Do not cross flooded streams on foot or bike.
